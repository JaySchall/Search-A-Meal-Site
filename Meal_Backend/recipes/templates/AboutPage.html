    Who Are We?

    We are JKGJ(Just Kidding Good Job), a small group of three college students working who created this website for a project in our Software Engineering II course. We use to have four people, which is how we came up with the group name JKGJ, which was made up of our first initals:

    Jonathan Schall || Kirk Caponpon || Gabe Gonzales || Jerome Marudo

    Unfortunately one of our members, Kirk, dropped out. He was the one who inspired and started the idea of making this website. He will be missed, but we hope we made him proud with how much work and how far we got in making his idea a reality.

    Our Project: Search a Meal

    Our project, Search a Meal, is a search engine for recipes. Users can simply search for recipes within our database using the search bar under Home. Users can create an account to store/save all the recipes they have come across. They can go back and view all their save/bookmarked recipes under the Account tab. If the user does not have an account, they can register for an account under the Login tab.
